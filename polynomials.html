Polynomials

A polynomial is an algebraic expression made up of variables, constants, and exponents combined using addition, subtraction, and multiplication.

Types of Polynomials

1. Monomial

A polynomial with only one term.

Example: 5x, 3y²

2. Binomial

A polynomial with exactly two terms.

Example: x + y, 3x² - 2x

3. Trinomial

A polynomial with exactly three terms.

Example: x² + y² + z², x² + 2x + 1

4. Polynomial of Higher Degree

Polynomials with more than three terms.

Example: x³ + 2x² + x + 5

5. Degree of a Polynomial

The degree is the highest exponent of the variable in a polynomial.

Example: Degree of 4x³ + 3x² - 5 is 3
