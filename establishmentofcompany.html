Establishment of Company Rule in India

1600: East India Company Founded

The English East India Company was established to trade in India, initially focusing on spices and textiles.

1757: Battle of Plassey

Company forces defeated Siraj-ud-Daulah, establishing political control in Bengal.

1764: Battle of Buxar

Company consolidated its rule in Bengal, Bihar, and Odisha after defeating Indian rulers.
