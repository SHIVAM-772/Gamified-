Class 9 English - Odisha Board

The Bond of Love

"The Bond of Love" is a heartwarming story that emphasizes the importance of family relationships, trust, and understanding. It tells the story of how family members support each other in difficult times, showing the emotional bond and care that make relationships strong and meaningful.

Missile Man of India

"Missile Man of India" is about Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, who contributed immensely to India's missile development program. The story highlights his journey from a small town to becoming the President of India, inspiring young minds to dream big, work hard, and serve the nation with dedication and honesty.
