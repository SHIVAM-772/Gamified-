Nationalist Movements & Revolutions in America & Europe

American Revolution

1775-1783, colonies fought against British rule; resulted in the independence of the USA.

French Revolution

1789-1799, led to overthrow of monarchy, rise of Napoleon, and modern democratic ideals.

Italian Unification

19th century movement to unify separate states into the Kingdom of Italy under leaders like Garibaldi and Cavour.

German Unification

Led by Otto von Bismarck in 1871, uniting German states into the German Empire.

1848 Revolutions

Wave of liberal and nationalist revolutions across Europe demanding democracy and national unification.
