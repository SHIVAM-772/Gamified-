Resistance to British Rule in India

Revolt of 1857

Also known as the First War of Independence, it was the major uprising against the British East India Company.

Tribal Uprisings

Tribes in Chota Nagpur, Munda and Santhal regions resisted exploitation by British revenue policies.

Peasant Movements

Farmers revolted due to high taxes and zamindari exploitation in different parts of India.

Reformist & Revolutionary Movements

Freedom movements emerged through moderate reforms and revolutionary groups seeking independence.
