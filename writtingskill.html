Class 9 English Writing Skills

Letter Writing

Formal Letter: Used for official purposes. Example structure:

Sender's Address
Date
Receiver's Address
Subject
Salutation
Body
Closing

Informal Letter: Used to write to friends/family. Example: Start with "Dear [Name]", end with "Yours lovingly".

Message Writing

Messages are short notes conveying information.

Example: "Meet me at the library at 5 PM. Don't be late. - Riya"

Tips: Keep it concise, clear, and informative.

Essay Writing

Essays are structured pieces of writing on a topic.

Structure: Introduction, Body, Conclusion.

Example Topic: "Importance of Education"

Story Writing

Story writing requires creativity and structure.

Structure: Beginning, Middle, End

Example: Moral story or personal experience story.

Notice Writing

Used to inform people about events or instructions.

Example Structure: Heading, Date, Time, Place, Purpose
