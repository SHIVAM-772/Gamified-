Class 9 Science - Physics Units

1. Motion

Motion describes the change in position of an object with respect to time. It includes concepts like:

  • Distance and Displacement
  • Speed and Velocity
  • Acceleration
  • Equations of Motion

Watch a related video: Motion Video

2. Force and Laws of Motion

Force can cause an object to move, stop, or change direction. Key laws include:

  • Newton’s First Law: Law of Inertia
  • Newton’s Second Law: F = ma
  • Newton’s Third Law: Action-Reaction

Try an interactive game: Newton’s Laws Simulation

3. Gravitation

Gravitation is the attractive force between two masses. Topics include:

  • Universal Law of Gravitation
  • Acceleration due to Gravity
  • Free Fall and Mass-Weight Relationship

Watch a video: Gravitation Video

4. Work and Energy

Work and energy describe how forces affect motion and the ability to perform work.

  • Work done = Force × Distance
  • Energy: Kinetic and Potential
  • Law of Conservation of Energy
  • Power and Units

Interactive simulation: Work & Energy Simulation

5. Sound

Sound is a wave motion perceived by our ears. Topics include:

  • Production and Propagation of Sound
  • Speed of Sound
  • Characteristics of Sound Waves: Pitch, Loudness, and Quality
  • Reflection of Sound and Echoes
