Impact of British Rule in India

Economic Impact

British policies led to deindustrialization, drain of wealth, and famines in India.

Political Impact

Centralized administration was established, princely states lost autonomy, and British control expanded.

Social Impact

Introduction of Western education, social reforms, and emergence of a new middle class.

Cultural Impact

Western culture influenced literature, architecture, and lifestyle; Indian traditions changed in some areas.
